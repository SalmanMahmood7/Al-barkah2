About Us

Al Barkah Paints & Coatings is one of Pakistan's most trusted and rapidly growing paint and coating solution providers.

Founded in 2018, the company has built a strong reputation for quality, innovation, and customer trust across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Initially, Al Barkah collaborated with leading national brands like Diamond, Brighto, Berger, My Paints, and Nippon, delivering high-standard projects under their systems. In 2023, the company proudly launched its own independent brand, offering complete coating solutions designed to meet local conditions and customer expectations.

Today, Al Barkah Paints & Coatings serves clients across Pakistan — from homes and offices to universities, hospitals, and industrial complexes — delivering excellence, reliability, and beauty in every stroke.
